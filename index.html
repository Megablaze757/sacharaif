About Us

Based in Sussex, we create bespoke joinery that transforms spaces with quality craftsmanship.

Crafting Excellence in Sussex

At 203 Carpentry, we blend traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design to create exceptional woodwork. Our skilled artisans take pride in every detail, ensuring each piece is not just furniture, but a work of art.

Whether it's custom cabinetry, elegant windows and doors, or bespoke bedroom furniture, we work closely with our clients throughout Sussex to bring their vision to life. See our work on Instagram for our latest projects.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of bespoke joinery services tailored to your specific needs.

Built-in Cabinetry & Media Walls

Floor-to-ceiling bespoke units with integrated lighting, panelled doors and oak shelving — designed to make a statement in any living space.

Bathroom Joinery & Panelling

Bespoke wall panelling, wainscoting and fitted joinery that bring character and craftsmanship to bathrooms of every style.

Doors & Windows

Handcrafted timber sash windows, bay window surrounds and bespoke doors that honour period architecture while meeting modern standards.
